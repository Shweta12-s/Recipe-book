🍰 Chocolate Cake

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk

Preheat oven to 180°C.

Mix all dry ingredients.

Add eggs and milk and blend well.

Bake for 30 minutes.

🍝 Creamy Pasta

  • 200g pasta
  • 1 cup cream
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, herbs
  • Grated cheese

Boil pasta until soft.

Heat olive oil, add cream.

Mix pasta, add cheese and herbs.

Serve hot.

🥞 Fluffy Pancakes

  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Mix all ingredients in a bowl.

Heat pan and pour batter.

Flip after bubbles form.

Cook till golden brown.

🍔 Veg Burger

  • Burger buns
  • Veg patty
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion
  • Mayonnaise, ketchup
  • Cheese slice

Toast buns and cook patty.

Add sauces to the buns.

Layer patty, veggies, and cheese.

Cover and serve warm.

🍓 Fruit Smoothie

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp honey

Add all ingredients to a blender.

Blend until smooth.

Pour into a glass and chill.

Serve cold with mint garnish.

🍚 Fried Rice

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • Chopped veggies
  • Soy sauce
  • Oil, garlic
  • Salt, pepper

Heat oil, sauté garlic and veggies.

Add rice and mix well.

Pour soy sauce and season.

Cook on high for 2-3 mins.
